What are earnings reports?
Every 3 months companies report how much money they made. Stocks often move 5-20% on earnings day. Tip: close your position before earnings if you're not experienced — the move can go either way regardless of how the chart looks.

How to export from Fidelity
1. Log in at fidelity.com
2. Go to Accounts & Trade → History
3. Set your date range and click Download
4. Choose CSV format
5. Upload the file below
Required CSV columns
Your CSV must have these column headers (case-insensitive):

Symbol, Action, Quantity, Price, Date

Action should be Buy or Sell. Matching Buy+Sell pairs for the same ticker will be auto-paired as a round-trip trade.
