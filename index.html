What are earnings reports?
Every 3 months companies report how much money they made. Stocks often move 5-20% on earnings day. Tip: close your position before earnings if you're not experienced — the move can go either way regardless of how the chart looks.

Welcome to TradeIQ
Your AI-powered trading companion

TradeIQ helps you learn to trade, analyze stocks with AI, track your trades, and practice with paper money — all in one place. No real money needed to get started.

Get started in 3 steps
1
Add your free API keys in Settings
You need two free keys to unlock live prices and AI analysis. Go to Settings ⚙ in the sidebar after closing this.
finnhub.io — live stock prices  ·  alphavantage.co — AI analysis
2
Search any stock to get AI analysis
Type a ticker like AAPL, TSLA, or SPY in the search bar at the top and click Analyze. You'll get a live chart, technical indicators explained in plain English, and an AI chat to ask questions.
3
Practice with Paper Trading
Start with $10,000 of fake money in the Paper Trading section under Learn. Set a target and stop loss, and the app will automatically close your trade when either level is hit. AI gives you coaching feedback on every trade.
💡 Pro tip
Work through the Trading Lessons under the Learn section — 9 lessons that take you from complete beginner to placing your first real trade.
